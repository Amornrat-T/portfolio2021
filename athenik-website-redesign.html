User Research

My team analyzed our target group and found that most of the users are age group 18 and over who are interested in studying abroad for a bachelor's degree to a doctorate degree.

User workflow and Sitemap

I plan the work of users since searching for courses to applying to universities and colleges by rearranging sitemap to make it easy to navigate.

Wireframe

Bring the designed wireframe and talk to the working team. To find conclusions, problems and fix them.

Prototype

Make a prototype from a modified wireframe and present it to CTO when approved, then develop it into a website.

Site map

When our team provides information on various content that should be put on the website. I started planning the sitemap. I was primarily concerned about the convenience of the user, So try to design it be as simple as possible. I started with the first page and put the important part that can links to other pages in the navigator bar.

Style Guide

I set the color scheme and font style to control the theme of the website. I need the style of the site to be the same for all current and future pages. Our style guide hence brings together styles that are accessible to all customers. So the blue color represents our target groups, agents or university, and the green color represents student groups. As for the typefaces, we chose to use them because it is not too formal and not too fun.

Don't stuff too much information!

In the preparation sitemap stage. We thought that for the convenience of the users, we would like to put all the details on one page, so the users don't have to go to another page to reach information. It turns out too much information, so we realized that adding a new page is nice as well.

Each people has their own perspective!

The design is beautiful or not depending on who sees it. Often during my work, my colleagues and I have conflicting opinions on the design of the website. I will probably have to edit my work multiple times to get the best out of it. But I also understand that sometimes my point of view is not always accurate.

Balancing is important!

Balancing the website is a necessary thing that we should concern about. I have to balance the graphics and content in the best balance possible. I focus on the distance between the content, the empty space in that section. If all contents are too close to make the website seem overgrown.
